IoT Environmental Sensor Board

Altium365

Designed a compact, low-power PCB for environmental monitoring in smart agriculture. Integrated temperature, humidity, and soil moisture sensors with a microcontroller and LoRa wireless module. Focused on robust power management and weatherproofing for outdoor deployment. The board supports remote firmware updates and real-time data streaming to a cloud dashboard. Used Altium365 for schematic capture, layout, and design review collaboration.

Robotics Motor Controller Board

Solidworks

Developed a high-efficiency motor controller PCB for a robotics platform. The board features dual H-bridge drivers, current sensing, and thermal protection. Designed for modularity and easy integration with various microcontrollers. Used Solidworks for enclosure design and mechanical fit checks. The project included extensive testing for reliability under continuous operation and is now used in a university robotics lab.
