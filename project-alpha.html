The Story

I built Talos AI from the ground up as a solo founder — every line of backend code, every customer conversation, every marketing campaign. In under three months since launching in November 2025, the platform hit a $60K/yr run-rate and is on track for $180K in its first year.

Talos automates business operations end-to-end, replacing hours of manual work with intelligent event-driven systems. I handled everything myself: architecting the infrastructure, engineering the data pipelines, building the product, acquiring customers, and scaling operations across 10+ business locations.

Technical Architecture

Event-Driven Backend

Architected with Redis orchestration, cutting end-to-end latency to <800ms and eliminating 90% of manual data entry across all client locations.

Fault-Tolerant ETL Pipelines

Engineered data pipelines that transform incoming streams into validated JSON via regex parsing, maintaining 99.9% data integrity at scale.

Parallel Processing

Decoupled event ingestion from Node.js backends, enabling parallel processing of 10K+ concurrent web requests without bottlenecks.

Vector Search Retrieval

Implemented semantic vector search to query live rosters in real time, ensuring 100% adherence to complex business constraints.

API & Transaction Integrity

Refined API logic and transaction boundaries to eliminate errors, scaling reliably to 10+ automated business locations.

Solo Full-Stack Execution

Built and shipped everything solo — backend infrastructure, product development, customer acquisition strategy, and go-to-market execution.
